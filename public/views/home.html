About Artisan

Welcome to a new a ever changing collection of unique handcrafted timeless furniture
and home decor at irresistible wholesale prices to the public. We aim to offer
the highest quality furniture at affordable prices.


Traveling extensively and working closely with the factories to develop and
acquire our furniture and home decor. World travel is how we find these
gems mainly from India, Indonesia, and China. Upon every visit you
will immerse yourself into a world of furniture, artisans, souks,
shipping lines, and travel. We love what we do and are proud of
our selection and quality. We search endlessly to provide the
best possible quality at the most reasonable price!
